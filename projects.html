Project # 1: Personal Website

Of course there's the obvious personal project: this website! I only had a few concrete ideas for the general layout of my page before creation since I find that creativity comes a lot more naturally after I got my mind in action. After I created my homescreen, I knew the general aesthetic I wanted to carry out with for the rest of the project. If my choice of font was reminding you of something familiar... SURPRISE! I used Minecraft's chatting font! As mentioned in my Personal section I played a few games and when I commited to the notepad/text vibe of my site, I thought what better than to use such a nostalgic font?

To the left is some rough diagram I sketched out after figuring out the basics of my homepage. The drawings helped me break down the site into easily digestible elements to implement. One of the sketches I had the most fun with would be my Personal page. I really liked marking out all the different nested flexboxes I needed to use. There is joy in watching a vision form into reality through pure time and effort.

I used HTML, CSS, JS, and animated with @ keyframes in this project. It was really fun working on this and has definitely pushed Web Development into my scope of passions/interests!
