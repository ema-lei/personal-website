Hi there! I'm Ema Lei, a 19 year old sophmore at UW, and this is my website. The image to the left and the page icon are all drawings of my favorite pokemon, Piplup. I love penguins and loved piplup ever since I was in third grade and it never really changed.

I enjoy making things, be it a little trinket, decorating a jar, or a coding project. One of my favorite aspects of creation is giving it my own personal flare. The font in this site is taken directly from a game thats followed me throughout my years growing up. There are little easter eggs in the pages too!

Crafting

Its always so fun getting losing track of time making a new trinket. I love learning through hands on experiences and I'm pretty sure my enjoyment in crafting plays some hand in that. While artistry isn't my strongest suit, I still work to the best of my limits and that hasn't managed to stop the fun yet so lets see how it goes!

Gaming

I love gaming! It's a great way to spend time with friends even across distances or to provide a short reprieve when reality gets a little too hard to handle. A couple of my favorites are: Minecraft, Baldur's Gate 3, Onmyoji, Lethal Company (or similar genres), and Palworld. Minecraft is a funny one. I usually pick it up for two weeks and then drop it for a year, but it has always been a safe game to fall back to when my friends and I have enough time.

Biking

Without giving out too many perilous details, I would say my enjoyment in biking has a lot to do with the convienent... shape of my neighborhood. Since I's not on a road, I can bike as slow or fast as I want and even listen to music when my headphones are on transparency mode. It's a great activity to give my mind a rest and my body a task, perfect for sandwiching between long study periods.

Fashion

Using the extra bits of time college gave me, I started to play around with my closet and explore diverses styles in hopes of finding one I liked. Eventually I did, finding comfort in the protective layers of buisness casual outfits. I must say overhauling your wardrobe takes a [no clicky] amount of time and a [i said no] amount of money.
