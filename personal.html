Hi there! I'm Ema Lei, a 19 year old sophmore at UW, and this is my website. The image to the left and the page icon are all drawings of my favorite pokemon, Piplup. I love penguins and loved piplup ever since I was in third grade and it never really changed.

I enjoy making things, be it a little trinket, decorating a jar, or a coding project. One of my favorite aspects of creation is giving it my own personal flare. The font in this site is taken directly from a game thats followed me throughout my years growing up. There are little easter eggs in the pages too!
